GateWay

Explore Gateway National Recreation Area for a nearby escape. Spanning New York and New Jersey, this urban oasis offers thrilling adventures and relaxing activities. Go for a bike ride, hike historic trails, or enjoy bird watching. With miles of beaches, you can swim, fish, or simply soak up the sun. Discover your next great outdoor adventure close to home!


Main Attractions of the Gateway Arch

  • The Gateway Arch: Take a tram ride to the top of this 630-foot monument for panoramic views.
  • Museum at the Gateway Arch: Explore six galleries under the Arch that tell the story of westward expansion.
  • The Old Courthouse: Visit the site where the pivotal Dred Scott case was heard.
  • Riverboat Cruises: See the Arch and the St. Louis skyline from the Mississippi River on a replica 19th-century steamboat.
  • "Monument to the Dream" Documentary: Watch a film about the design and construction of the Gateway Arch.
  • National Park Grounds: Enjoy over 90 acres of green space with walking paths and gardens.
